For compatibility with Minetest Game and mods made for it, it is recommended that you either use the compatiblity layer that is not distributed on Luanti ContentDB, or alternatively you can copy all the contents from the minetest game's directory on your file system, that is, where the "default" resides along with it's friends, either into the "mods" directory here in betaclonia right there next to this file, or better, copy them to the regular mods directory ./minetest/mods. Such modification you make to your installation is not an official part of the betaclonia project and is your responsibility, betaclonia provides support for such compatibility features for those who wish to utilize them at their own discretion.
